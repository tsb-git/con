DMV Studio, and its new Production Service is award winning production company with offices in Kolkata , Mumbai , Delhi . Established in 2014, DMV Studio produces Feature Films, TV Commercials, Documentaries and Photography Campaigns. Through its Production Services arm FILM IN INDIA, we also provide production support in the Indian Sub Continent. DMV Studio represents a group of worldclass talent that excel in their diverse approaches to storytelling, visual styles, performance genres. These carefully selected directors & photographers from across the globe are known for their creative excellence and award winning works. Our experienced, skillful and committed production team will then strive to support these talents to get the best out of them and turn a vision into a reality on image

Our aim is make a project and vision “the best it can be” – through an experience that is smooth and enjoyable but with results that are unique, successful and momentous.

                                                            What We Do

With over 10+ films/short flims as part our showreel, DMV Studio has produced TV commercials for several brands – some of which have won us accolades. Our roster of talents include award winning directors, directors of photography, art directors and editors. By mutually selecting the best talents to execute your stories and suit your ideas, our goal is to transform the clients vision into fully realized ground breaking work. Uncompromising quality, passionate creativity, precise and transparent budgeting along with an experienced, committed and skillful crew will help us achieve this transformation.

The main objective of the feature film division of our company is to produce or coproduce Asian themed stories for the international market. To find intriguing stories, develop them into scripts and finally transform them into an unforgettable experience on the silver screen. 

DMV Studio is the new subsidiary production service. We have line produced feature films, TV commercials, corporate videos, documentaries and music videos for several reputed companies. We provide preproduction, production, and post production services in the Indian subcontinent . We are supported a multilinguage and state of the art equipment that meet some of the highest international standards. If your films needs it we will help you find it.
